    How it works

    Robots roam a 50x50 grid. Each robot has 16 genes that determine its sensing, combat, mobility, and efficiency stats. Power use matters: heavier bodies and big engines cost energy every cycle.

    • Move: random neighboring cell, unless occupied.
    • Mate or attack: if a robot is in the target cell.
    • Combat rewards fitness; mining yields ore and energy.
    • Robots shut down after hitting max age.

    Use the overlays to spot trails, mining hotspots, and combat flashes. Clicking a robot reveals its genes and derived stats.
